About Me

I’m Raven, a web developer based in Charlotte, NC. I consider myself a lifelong learner who continuously lives outside my comfort zone. After several years in the real estate finance industry, my newest passion is coding. I always wanted to become fluent in a second language. I just didn’t know back then that language would be computer programming.

Outside of coding, I have an intense love and passion for food. If I’m not enjoying a good meal with my family or friends, I can usually be found reading, meditating, or playing!

I have a BA from the University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill and a MBA from North Carolina State University.
